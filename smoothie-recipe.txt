Ingredients:
 - 1/4 cup coconut water
 - 1/4 cup pomegranite juice
 - 1 sliced banana
 - 1 cup blueberries
 - 1 cup strawberries
 - 1 cup kale
 - 1 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
 - 2 Tbsp almond slivers
 - 1 Tsp bee pollen
 - 1/2 avocado

Directions:
 1. Add ingredients to blender in top to bottom order
 2. Blend until smooth
 3. Mix with yogurt if desired.
